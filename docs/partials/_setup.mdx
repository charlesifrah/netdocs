- **Register in the Developer Portal**

  To get started with Hubble Network, you must first register an account on the [Developer Portal](https://dev.hubblenetwork.com).

- **Create an Organisation**

  After creating your account on Hubble Network, proceed to create an organization. All sessions and logs are managed within your organization.

- **Get your API Keys**

  Copy the [API Keys](https://dev.hubblenetwork.com/apikeys) for your organization from the Developer Portal. Your API Key is unique to your organization and is required as an authorization header when using Hubble Network's REST APIs.
